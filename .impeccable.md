## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary Audience**: Engineering Managers, Technical Recruiters, and Direct Clients.
- **Viewing Context**: Desktops (professional workstations during recruitment reviews) and mobile devices (quick-scans during tech events or social shares).
- **Core Goal**: Instantly assess Guido Naselli's technical capability, quality of work, professional background, and developer polish.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Rigorous, Mechanical, Intentional.
- **Tone**: Professional, precise, and understated. We avoid empty superlatives ("next-gen", "cutting-edge") and display clean, dense, verified technical metrics.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Artsy-Utilitarian. Clean slate layouts, razor-sharp geometric alignment, high whitespace ratio, and organic materials (true frosted glass, fine-grid background textures).

### Active Dials
- **`DESIGN_VARIANCE: 8`**: Asymmetric spacing, left-aligned titles (no centered headers), offset media elements, and modular grid structure (Bento 2.0). High fallback security on viewports < 768px.
- **`MOTION_INTENSITY: 6`**: Snappy spring physics (`type: "spring", stiffness: 100, damping: 20`), staggered waterfall entries, layout morphs, and isolated perpetual micro-interactions.
- **`VISUAL_DENSITY: 4`**: Art gallery spacing. Generous whitespace, no nested cards, clean lines to separate sections instead of floating boxes, letting data breathe.

### Design Principles
1. **Typography as Structure**: Reject default and reflex fonts. Use `Geist` and `Geist Mono` in a highly contrasted modular scale to let typography define sections, rather than using colored lines or card containers.
2. **Desaturated Accentuation**: Keep color usage pure and restricted. The interface uses a single calibrated desaturated Emerald/Teal accent (`oklch(0.65 0.14 160)`) on top of deep Slate neutral shades (`zinc-950` / `zinc-50`). No purple/blue neon gradients or glowing shadows.
3. **Tactile & Material Sensation**: Interactive elements must behave like physical devices—snappy `:active` push-downs, and menus constructed with "Liquid Glass" refraction borders (`border-white/10` / `border-black/5`) and inner reflections.
4. **Content Density over Cliché Cards**: Never wrap elements in repeated floating cards. Group content naturally using top borders, Negative Space, or clean metadata columns. Numbers and dates are formatted strictly in monospace (`font-mono`) to enhance the mechanical feel.
